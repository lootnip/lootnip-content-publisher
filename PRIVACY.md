# Privacy Policy

## 1. Overview

Lootnip Content Publisher is designed to help users prepare and manage social media publishing workflows.

## 2. Data We May Process

Depending on the connected platform and workflow, the service may process:

- connected account identifiers,
- access credentials or tokens,
- post captions and metadata,
- media file references,
- publishing status and response logs.

## 3. Purpose of Processing

This data is used only to:

- prepare publishing requests,
- upload and publish authorized content,
- track publishing status,
- support troubleshooting and workflow management.

## 4. Data Sharing

This service does not sell personal data.

Data may be transmitted only to the platforms that the user explicitly authorizes for content publishing or account connection.

## 5. Security

Reasonable efforts should be made to protect stored credentials and operational data.

## 6. User Control

Users remain responsible for managing their connected accounts, uploaded content, and platform permissions.

## 7. Updates

This policy may be revised as the service changes.

## 8. Contact

A public contact method may be added after the official repository and service profile are finalized.
